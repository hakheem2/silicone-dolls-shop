About
Caroline Heuss

At Caroline Heuss Silicone Dolls, every creation is more than a doll — it’s a story, a memory, and a piece of art. As a passionate, family-run studio, we craft ultra-realistic, handcrafted silicone baby dolls using platinum-grade medical silicone for unmatched texture and lifelike presence.
From carefully hand-painted skin tones to intricately rooted hair, each doll is designed to offer a comforting, heartwarming experience. Whether you're a collector, grieving parent, or first-time enthusiast, our babies are made to be cherished.
